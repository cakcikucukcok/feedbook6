Journey Map is a process of transformation, of burning through the blocks that keep us trapped in fear, to experience a new life of expanded awareness and joy. Its a map to the hidden treasure within.

Along the path of our lives, we lose parts of ourselves when we experience trauma or are exposed to the false stories of others. Once those parts are lost, we experience fear because we are not whole. Something is missing. Many ancient traditions have ceremonies to recover those lost parts, but in our modern world, the majority of us do not have access to them. Journey Map is soul retrieval for the rest of us.

This is a map to you! Its a guide to valuing yourself and loving yourself for the powerful potential that you are.

You embody the greatness of the universe.
