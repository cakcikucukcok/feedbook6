THE WAY OF THE SOUL

How to Create Instant Magic in a Busy Life

We all tend to live such busy lives, with no time for ourselves or one another.

Bring the magic into your life.

This book is designed as an educational tool to help you understand human behavior.

As you read these insightful messages, you will begin to explore and understand new concepts and new viewpoints on the human condition.

Each message carries a compelling heartfelt intention for you to feel at the core of your being.

This intention is amplified with a new command statement labeled The Message and You.

Life as you know it will transform like a caterpillar transforms into a beautiful butterfly; you will also begin to remember to embrace, reconnect, and re-ignite the brilliance of your true essence.

Ross Bonacci
