Your choice: Fear or faith. How are you going to handle the increasing stresses, fears and uncertainties of your life? You can choose to live in fear or you can choose to live in Faith. Easy to say, but how do you actually choose the high road when the world is chaotic and the future is unpredictable? At last - Dr. Joe ""Mr. Fire!"" Vitale steps in and offers a no-nonsense inspiring view of how to live from love and confidence. His no-punches-pulled approach to helping you will fortify and guide you. He discusses Faith in yourself, Faith in others, Faith in the world, and Faith in a Higher Power. This is not a book based on ""blind Faith"" - instead, this book is filled with examples and stories of Faith at work. You'll quickly read this breezy book, be able to base it on something real and change your life for the better, easily and effortlessly.
