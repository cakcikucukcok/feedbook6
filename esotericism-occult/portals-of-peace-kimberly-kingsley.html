The desire for world peace is a deeply held value shared by all of humanity. Sometimes the cry for peace gets louder and sometimes it fades to a hum, but it still stands as an important issue. In todays world, a diverse group of people are working toward world peace. In Portals of Peace, author Kimberly Kingsley presents a radical approach to healing the worldone person at a time. In order to facilitate peace in the world, we must first have it within ourselves.

This step-by-step guide to inner and outer peace introduces the concept of essentialization, which is the process of embodying ones essence while becoming a bright light in the world. The five stages of essentialization help us shed the layers covering our essence, leading to a completely unique and powerful presence.

Portals of Peace demonstrates that the pursuit of inner peace is no longer a lofty ideal, but a way to bring about real change in the world. As we open from the inside and allow our light to fully shine, a new era of peace will blanket the world.
