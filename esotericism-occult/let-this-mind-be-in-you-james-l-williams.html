Mans plight in this world of physical existence is due in part to his inability to recognize and understand the source, and purpose of all that exists in it. Spiritual laws are the driving force shaping and determining the character and nature of all things. A careful look into scripture helps us to understand that all that has been created, be it the simple microcosm of the atom, or the more complex macrocosm of the stars and planets hold a unique place in understanding ourselves. Even the character of man is governed by laws and principles originating beyond the natural self.

LET THIS MIND BE IN YOU is a comprehensive biblical and scientific study into how behavior is governed by laws and natures, revealing insight into creation and the purpose for which all exists. All of creation has the unique ability to reveal the character and nature of its creator. For the invisible things of Him from the creation of the world are clearly seen, being understood by the things that are made, even his eternal power and Godhead; so that they are without excuse (Romans 1:20).
