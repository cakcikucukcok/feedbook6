Who told you that you were naked? This volume is my response to Gods equipping. As you read Wisdom Nuggets: Sermons, Essays, Inspirational Thoughts, and Affirmations, you will find my transparency very revealing in what is intended to be a call to action.

You will want to keep this volume at your finger tips because the collective materials are essential reading and can be used on any occasion. You will come back to this resource over and over for the treasures you will find between each page. Wisdom Nuggets is more than a compilation of sermons, essays, inspirational thoughts, and affirmations; it is a working tool that provides awe-inspiring challenges and is a thought-provoking, practical guide for daily use for every situation.

Learn how to plumb deeper depths in relationships with God and Gods creations as you apply these wisdom nuggets. Create your own opportunity for change by embracing strategic models for self-awareness and positive motivation.
