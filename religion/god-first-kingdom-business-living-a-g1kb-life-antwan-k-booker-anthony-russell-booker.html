You can’t control life’s circumstances, but you can control how  you live.

In John 10:10, Jesus says He came so that we might live abundantly. All who obey His commands are guaranteed eternal life. However, one does not have to wait until eternity to get the best that Jesus has to offer.

God First Kingdom Business (G1KB) is a tool that both Christians and non-Christians can use. Believers will be challenged to grow in every area of their lives as they look at their own spiritual reflections and evaluate whether or not the image of Christ is staring back at them. For the non-believer, especially one looking to “fill a void” in life, this resource will bring clarity about what it means to be saved and how God intends for His children to live on earth.

Whether you’re a seasoned Christian, a new believer, or one not quite sure what you believe, the lessons in this book, if applied, can help you maximize the potential you have to live your best now!  G1KB isn’t simply a book; it’s a lifestyle!
