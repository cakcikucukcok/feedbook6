Science has never been able to explain why we cant see God with our physical eyes, even though Heor some mysterious powercreated the universe, is maintaining the universe, and someday may destroy the universe.

God also created man on earth, and its up to us to find Him and understand His energy, power and secretsand a team of doctors, scientists, and gurus do just that in this exploration of physics, spirituality, and how the two intertwine. They answer questions such as: What are the physics behind prayer and meditation? How can faith, prayer, and God help us raise and educate children? How can we raise children with advanced minds and souls? What is the nature of the relationship between God and man?

The authors also explain how God carries out justice, how the mind is like an atomwith huge amounts of energy trapped inside, how an atomic model of family can reduce and eliminate divorce, and how to promote good mental health.

Spiritual and materialistic sciences collide in the Physics of God, Universe, Humankind, and Peace in Family. Father is the Heaven, Father is the Religion Father should be worshipped.
