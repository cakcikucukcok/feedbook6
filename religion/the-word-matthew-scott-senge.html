This book was created with the intent to grab and wake you from the message of the modern-day church that preaches God has a wonderful plan for your life or a lifestyle-enhancement Gospel. Imagine hearing these words from our Holy Creator on Judgment Day: Depart from me you who practice lawlessness, I never knew you. What would you do if that happened? Moreover, are you willing to take that chance? Examine yourself right now. Do you consider yourself a good person, really? Romans 3:10 (NKJV) states, There is none righteous, no, not one. Take a closer look at the beliefs you may have been taught throughout your life.
