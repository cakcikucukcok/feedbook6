Do you want to make better choices in life? "The Power of Choice" is an extraordinary masterpiece that is the perfect tool that empowers people from all walks of life to make better decisions. Life is not a game of chance but rather a matter of choice. We often see these choices as isolated events instead of connecting the dots between the choices we make and the things we experience.  In this book, Bishop Wright teaches that our choices, whether good or bad, will have a ripple effect on our life and often those that are connected to us.  He emphasizes the need to maintain a positive attitude even when we are faced with adversity and hardship.  Our words are powerful, and Bishop Wright reminds us to choose wisely what we say, especially in the pivotal moments in our life. There is a consequence to every choice that you make- some minor, others monumental- “Therefore choose life, that both thou and thy seed may live:”
