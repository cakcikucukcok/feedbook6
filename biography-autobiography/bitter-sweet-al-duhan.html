After escaping from a tortured childhood in Connecticut, I left home at a young age and traveled to California. The torture had followed me where ever I went. There was a battle between the officer and I but was able to escape. I was captured and incarcerated for the robbery that I had no knowledge of. While I was serving a two year sentence, my fiance Bonnie, was raped and became pregnant with his child. She pleaded for me to stay, but not having employment, I must return to Connecticut. We were talking about her baby and I told her I could still love you but I could never love the baby. Then I heard strange noises. She was punching her stomach and screaming, I will kill it I will kill it for you. I screamed back at her I had nothing to do with this. But I knew I should have never said that. Then I had a nightmare, that I went back to California to see Bonnie, I was able to see that she was lying with her hand in a large pool of blood, Why God, Why her! Was this a Paranormal Migration.
