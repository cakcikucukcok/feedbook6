Mama no, I don't want to do that!

SEXTOY is a riveting new memoir, a story of incest survival that you won't soon forget. The chilling truth about the chameleon that lurks within a family.

September 30, 1943 was the day, Micheline, entered a private clinic where her son was born. He was named Jean Marie but later he came to use the name Daniel. Jean Marie was born in Paris into a wealthy, bourgeois family, and into a social class where image and reputation were everything. Micheline was a stylish, classy, socially prominent young woman, but behind that image she did what ever she needed to do in order to protect her dirty little secret.    

This true story will sweep you across two continents, beginning in Auvergne, a rustic, unspoiled region of France, to Paris, the city of light and enchantment, and on to North America, where a young man tries to run away from who he was... a sextoy.
