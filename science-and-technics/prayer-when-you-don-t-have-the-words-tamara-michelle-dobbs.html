PrayerWhen You Dont Have the Words is a simple and easy-to-use prayer book that can help us bare our souls to God, even in our most troubled times or when we dont know how to pray. Whether we need prayers for ourselves or for others, author and healer Tamara Michelle Dobbs provides a collection of inspiring and powerful prayers that can raise our words to the heavens. The prayers included can help us find the right words to ask for prosperity, clarity, joy, and peace, and they can help us express our desires to bless our children, find comfort amid caretaking, death, and loss, and request guidance and protection for neighbors, servicemen, and political leaders.

Prayer can provide us with a way to glorify our Creator and find guidance on our spiritual journeys, and it can also enable us to help others overcome obstacles and find healing. Discover how to pray effectively and sincerely, finding within the words necessary to offer thanksgiving, comfort, and peace.
