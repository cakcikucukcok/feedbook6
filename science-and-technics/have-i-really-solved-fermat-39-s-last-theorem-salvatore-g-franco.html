The author, after an unusual incipit and a brief mention to the complex demonstration of the Theorem, elaborated by the great mathematician Andrew Wiles, wants to try himself in the im-pari tenzone, but using only white weapons, within everyone's reach. His reasoning, simple and intuitive, runs along a road that, by his own admission, still needs more enlightened confirmations. But hope is his companion and Research is the ultimate goal of life, even if, in the back cover, he feels the danger of pride, inherent in many manifestations of the human soul.
