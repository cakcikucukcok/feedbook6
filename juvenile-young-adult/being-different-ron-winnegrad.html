This is a book about being different and having the courage to embrace that difference and accepting difference and originality in others.

Being Different is about accepting yourself for your difference and in turn accepting others. Being different does not mean one is bad or inferior. Being different can mean being on a different path or mindset. It is about having the confidence to walk a different path, in finding comfort in not being or doing like others.

Being different should give you the strength to stand tall, rely on your beliefs and not be upset if your socks dont match!

Embrace everyone and everything, even if they are different. It is a chance to show you something new. Many successful people become so because they looked at being different. You can go to a different level by being different.
