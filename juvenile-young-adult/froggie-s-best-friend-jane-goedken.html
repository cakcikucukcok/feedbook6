Froggie sits in his pond by the tall green grass, wondering who will be his best friend. For advice, he goes to his cousin, a tree frog, who tells him that if he looks closely, there are many friends around who care.

Froggie decides to keep watching for his best friend. As the days go by, lots of animals, birds, and insects stop by the pond, including humans, ducks, butterflies, turtles, and many others. But none of them seem quite right to be Froggies best friend. Will Froggie ever find someone special to spend his time with?

In this childrens story filled with colorful pictures and rhymes, a frog searching for a best friend meets many creatures stopping by his pond and discovers friendship in an unexpected place.
