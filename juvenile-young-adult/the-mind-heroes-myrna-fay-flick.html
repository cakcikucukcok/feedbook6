Enlighten and Empower Children

After all, they will be our future leaders and they need our help! The statistics on bullying, anxiety, depression, and suicide continue to climb. Many children feel numb, misunderstood, lonely, confused, and are scared.They wonder if they have a place on this earth. It’s time for all children to feel valued, appreciated and loved. Their social and emotional health needs to be nourished. Help them discover the power of their own minds. 

Parents are overworked and overwhelmed. Sometimes they too feel lost and don’t know how to connect with their own personal power. The Mind Heroes is a tale and a tool to connect with emotion, heart, and soul to your children.

Val, our heroin creates a fantasy world with characters/friends who live in the clouds. They lead her on an adventure of self-discovery and empowerment. Throughout the journey, Val inspires the new family in town to change their ways. Kindness, inclusion, compassion and the desire to live a great life wins in the end.

In addition to life lessons weaved into the story, there are six key concepts. When discussed with your child thesesix simple, yet multi-faceted words will make a positive and profound difference in their emotional and social wellbeing! (Visit www.TheMindHeroes.com for more details.)

The 6 words:

  1. Believe
  2. Learn
  3. Practice
  4. Choose
  5. Connect 
  6. Enjoy
