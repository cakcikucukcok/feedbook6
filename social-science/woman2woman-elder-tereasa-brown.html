Men and women both face challenges in relationships, problems with insecurities, and self-esteem issues. Woman2Woman addresses these issues candidly. Women will be able to determine if theyve been cheating themselves by cheapening their value and selling themselves short, all the while appreciating the value of respect for a man and themselves. This book makes you laugh, makes you cry, makes you say hmmm, and definitely makes you take an inventory of your self-worth. Anyone reading this book can relate one way or another. Men and women alike will be conversing about this book throughout the ages.
