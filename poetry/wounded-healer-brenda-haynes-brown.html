Wounded Healer is a release of lyrical Psalms and poetry penned by Brown since her youth. She has recorded her questions, sentiments and perceptions that reflect a variety of personal life experiences and dealings with God.

Written in times of doubt, repentance, and trials, these Psalms are to hold close to the heart, as a "gift", while Child and Father are "working things out". They will serve as a personal lifeline and spiritual compass for those navigating their own infinite journeys.

The Psalms are simplistic and flow from a heart who undeniably knows God in a deeply personal way. Soothing and calming, yet gutsy and raw, her lyrics flow from a tested, yet passionate, relationship with the Lord.

The Psalms are shared in poetry form, without their melody; for in their message lies the Hope and Power.

Brown did not compose these Psalms and poems as entertainment or for any great literary value. They are simply "Bread", multiplied and served from the leftover fragments of the broken pieces of her own insightful walk with Jesus.

These Psalms and spiritual songs stir us with hope that no matter how painful our circumstances or how tearstained our pillows, ultimately, Joy will come in the morning!
