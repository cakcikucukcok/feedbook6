Starting with the Psalms, poetry has long had a beloved and important place in Christian culture. Mark Anthony Grubb continues and maintains that tradition with this volume of poetry that will bring you to spiritual heights you have never experienced.

Grubb’s gifted hand weaves a gamut of cadenced expressions, including the cross of Jesus, death and dying, gratitude for moms, a celebration of nature, God’s promises expressed in the coming of spring, questioning whether Jesus ever laughed, honor for the flag, and many more. For the faithful, this book will encourage, enlighten and challenge. For others, a journey with Grubb’s verse will inspire you to contemplate your spiritual condition.

Lovers of inspirational lyrical poetry will find a treat in this classic hardback volume of 77 poems.
