“Do not die in the history of your past hurts and past experiences, but live in the now and future of your destiny.” - Michelle Obama

Being a singer, she had only previously ever written a few songs, and not really considered herself a poet. Totally shocked at receiving some kind of spiritual awakening in the form of words given to her in a semi-conscious state, Jenessa began writing. She embarked upon this new found talent to consciously write from the heart and share her true thoughts and feelings.

The Healing: Messages from Within, is a poetry collection of her inspirations, hopes, dreams and fears. With love, beauty, gratitude and at times despair, in a bid to combat the reoccurring bouts of depression that has plagued her for many years, The Healing: Messages From Within was born. This offering, which also includes her phenomenal story Slimspiration, is sassy, uplifting, thought-provoking and moving. Delve in and experience her life journey through the highs, lows, joys and sorrows.
