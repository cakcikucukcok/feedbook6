Whispers: Poetry from My Soul presents a collection of soulful glimpses into Carol Stone-Taylor's spiritual journey. God is our constant companion on our journeys. His loving-kindness is manifested in the joys of life and love along with the pain of sorrow and death. God's glory, blessings, mercy, and grace surround us and breathe life into us. As a child of God, Stone-Taylor celebrates the growth through which she is constantly discovering the presence of God in her life. Through her poetry, she expresses her awareness of His omnipotent presence in all aspects of life. The collected verses in Whispers: Poetry from My Soul are her revelations of God's awesome, loving nature and the reality of His Grace in her life.
