Her is a collection of poetry and prose that follows the Wanderer as she struggles to maintain her faith in the unseen God in the face of adversity.

Her takes readers through a raw journey of the reality most people of faith endure, but do not discuss. There are some bitter moments, but also moments of joy.

Highlighting experiences of anxiety, depression, rejection, faith, and surrender, along with beautiful sketches created specifically for this volume, Her is a journey of mountains and valleys that ultimately lead to triumph.
