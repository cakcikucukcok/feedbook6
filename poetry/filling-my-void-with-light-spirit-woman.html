From time to time, we may feel lost, in despair, engulfed in hopelessness. Regardless of how long and how serious this period may be, it affects all aspects of our lives. If it is not ourselves who experience these emotions, someone around us may. Filling My Void with Light, is a compelling personal journey of a woman who has the courage to speak her truth to heal herself as well as to help others in their healing journey. Spirit Woman shows through her poetry and prose that it is possible to do both. In fact, helping others can help us help ourselves. Get ready to travel through a womans courageous transformation. Filling my Void with Light includes writings to which many of us can relate. This book may bring a tear and chuckle in your belly at the same time. Her work is soulful, heartfelt and very inspirational.
