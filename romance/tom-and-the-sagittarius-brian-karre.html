Who is writing your horoscope? Tom and Leigh lived in a small mountain town about an hour outside of Yellowstone National Park. They met every morning at a coffee shop before work. When Tom found out that Leigh might be leaving his life, he knew that he had to do everything he could to try and stop her from going. And there was only one thing that Tom would be able to do to convince Leigh that she was not supposed to go. He had to make sure it was written in the stars.
