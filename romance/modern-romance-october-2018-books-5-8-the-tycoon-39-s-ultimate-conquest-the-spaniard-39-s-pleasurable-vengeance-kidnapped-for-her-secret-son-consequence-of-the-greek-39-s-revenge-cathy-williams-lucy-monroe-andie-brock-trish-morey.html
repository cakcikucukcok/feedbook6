The Tycoon's Ultimate Conquest by Cathy Williams

The Spaniard's Pleasurable Vengeance by Lucy Monroe

Ruthless tycoon Basilio Perez, famed for his familial loyalty, has a new target in sight. Miranda Smith is poised to bring the Perez name into disrepute – she must be stopped! But when he meets Miranda, Basilio is captivated by her innocence and shy appeal. To uncover Randi’s secrets, his plan for revenge becomes one of lingering, passionate seduction…that tests his iron control to the limit!

Kidnapped for Her Secret Son by Andie Brock

Billionaire Jaco Valentino is furious when Leah McDonald leaves him without explanation. But when he discovers Leah’s given birth to his heir he’s determined to shield them from his adoptive family’s wicked intentions! Jaco kidnaps Leah and his son, whisking them away to his remote Sicilian island for their own safety. Except the sinful flame still burning between him and Leah feels infinitely dangerous…

Consequence of the Greek's Revenge by Trish Morey

Athena Nikolides is wary of being exploited for her newly-inherited fortune. So she’s devastated to learn Alexios only wants her to avenge himself against her father! But when the consequence of their undeniable passion is revealed, now he wants her for so much more…
