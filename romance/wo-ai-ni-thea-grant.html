After being scammed into losing millions of dollars, Yu Mei Kwang’s father arranges a marriage for her to save his yacht business. She abhors the archaic process of arranged marriages, but surrenders, after deception by her father.

Desperate for a way out, she seeks the counsel of a wise man. They delve into the I Ching, and he tells her to look east for love, even though it defies her father.

American designer, Douglas O’Riley, arrives in Hong Kong on holiday. He’s determined to relax, get away from his responsibilities.

Yu Mei is drawn to Doug. After a clandestine rendezvous, she throws caution to the wind and balks at her father’s idea of marriage. She has no idea this will place her—and her father—in danger from her powerful betrothed.

Will following her heart entrap Yu Mei, Doug and her father, or set them all free?
