When Krystal McKenzie and her friend Donna were kidnapped and tortured by the Rattler Kings, Krystal was broken by the death of her friend.

She had no one to turn to or trust except her rescuer, Samuel. 

Samuel knew he loved her and would not allow her to be anything but happy.  Confident. Whole. So he took her home so he could care for her and keep her safe. 

The Rattler Kings decided she needed to die because she knew too much. 

Samuel vowed to protect her from danger no matter what.

He hadn't counted on Krystal making the mistake that put her right back into the hands of her sadistic enemies.

This is a story about an unconditional love that can’t be broken, a trust that can’t be shattered and a stubbornness that won’t take no for an answer.
