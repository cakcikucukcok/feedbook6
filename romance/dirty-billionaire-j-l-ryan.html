My name is Sierra, and I have a huge problem. I keep scraping the bottom of the barrel when it comes to men, and if I go out with one more sleazeball, I'll go crazy. Maybe I shouldn't be too picky, though. I'm 90 pounds overweight, broke, and desperate for a baby.

The good guys run the other way when they see me coming. My biological clock is ticking away, I'm frantic because I'm facing homelessness, and I'm so fat that I can barely breathe. What guy wouldn't be attracted to that? 

I'm A.J. I've always had to fight my way to the top, but I made it because I've got guts. I run my business like a crazy person. That's how I made my first billion at such a young age.

There's nothing I can't have. Women throw themselves at me, but there's only one problem. I'm not interested.

I've never wanted anything that was too easy for me to obtain. That includes women. I can possess anything and anyone, but not Sierra. Captivating, shy Sierra. So thrilling, so sensual. She doesn't want a relationship with me, but she has no choice...
