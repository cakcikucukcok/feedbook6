In this stunning new book, Keira Sykes takes us on an intellectual journey through the world of Clojure--the best and the brightest, the most famous and the most successful. Asking the question: what makes high Clojure achievers different?

Along the way unfolding the secrets of Clojure success, and what it takes to be a great Clojure player.

Brilliant and entertaining, 'The Clojure Handbook' is a landmark work that will simultaneously delight and illuminate. PLUS, INCLUDED with your purchase, are real-life document resources; this kit is available for instant download, giving you the tools to navigate and deliver on any Clojure goal.
