FOUR-BOOK BUNDLE

Every continent a sterile desert. Every ocean an acidic grave. Every food source a distant memory.

700 years After the Cycle of Extinctions, a doomed boy and girl hold the key to humanity’s survival. Can they save the species? Should they?

Readers are calling The Extinction Odyssey “storytelling at its most masterful” and “one of the most addictive” sci-fi series. Fans of classic dystopian tales like Soylent Green and Logan’s Runwill enjoy this modern twist on humanity’s last gasp.

Box Set #1 spans the first half of the series and includes FOUR heart-pounding titles:

Sound (Book 1)

Daoren and Heqet stand an even chance of celebrating their 20th birthdays. Life 700 years After the Cycle of Extinctions offers no better odds. In Daqin Guojin, every 19-year-old prospect for denizenship must sit the Survival Aptitude Test. Only the smartest pass. The rest are harvested for grooll, the city-state’s sole food source and barter currency.

If Daoren’s intellect can outpace his anxiety, he might earn the first perfect score in history and be named ruler. Heqet knows Daoren can’t do it without her—even if he’d be the last person to ask for help. But when tragedy strikes all-too-close to home, the pair make a horrifying discovery that binds their fate and crushes all hope.

Fury (Book 2)

Four stunned survivors must put the horrors of the grooll mill behind them to save their city-state. Pursued by merciless enemies, they battle their way out of Daqin Guojin and venture across the acidic Sea of Storms. Their objective? Track down a fabled food source in the Great Saharan Desert. The plan reeks of suicide, but the fate of humanity’s starving masses hangs in the balance.

Rise (Book 3)

After countless perils and heart-wrenching losses, the denizens of Daqin Guojin can finally look to a brighter future. But life 701 years After the Cycle of Extinctions is unpredictable. When an old enemy invades Daqin Guojin at the head of a mongrel army and a treacherous Asianoid sparks a bloody uprising to restore the S.A.T., the agents of progress find themselves trapped between two unstoppable forces.

Hope’s Graveyard (Bonus Prequel)

Laoshi's first mission as a Jiren couldn't be more daunting: infiltrate Havoc on the eve of a major offensive and decimate the mongrel colony’s command and control capabilities. It's enough to give the seasoned veterans on the assault team pause, let alone a raw recruit fresh from passing his S.A.T. Fortunately, he’ll be fighting alongside his best friend. The pair have been training hard for the mission . . . so hard they can read each other's thoughts. But neither could imagine the horrors awaiting them in Hope's Graveyard.
