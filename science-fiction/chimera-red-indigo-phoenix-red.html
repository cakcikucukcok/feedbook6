On Tychon, a planet rich with technology, conflict and and an elevated connection to music, two species battle for self preservation. After rotations of isolation living with her mother, ChimeraRED moves to a Red underground city known as AzurNu to find her missing father. Any hopes of Adjusting to AzurNu is shattered after Grey terrestrial forces led by Lord Prychon, issue a new decree. This decree reignites the Grey's campaign to find and slaughter all Reds who do not surrender for mental reconditioning. With stories of Reds disappearing at the hands of a mysterious Red syndicate and the threat of death from Grey attack, AzurNu may not be the safe haven that Chimera expected. As Chimera forges new relationships and embarks on epic adventures, she is introduced to a mysterious music creating society who call themselves the "Creators". Chimera is soon confronted with the decision of whether to use her unique gifts to fight the revolution with weapons of war or the power of creation?
