Grak And Other Short Stories is a collection of short stories by 16 year old Author, Zephan Wells. This book is a compilation of work accomplished while participating in the 2013 and 2014 NaNoWriMo Young Writer's Program. Grak and his goblin friends blow up their home town and land in a nearby human city where their adventures continue. Their super fix-it skills land them a job with a local blacksmith and ultimately Grak meets the king. What will happen next? Nintal wants to join the Paladin elite Aether's Guard. What does it take to pass the test? Follow along with these stories and more in this book of short stories. Support the work of a young writer!
