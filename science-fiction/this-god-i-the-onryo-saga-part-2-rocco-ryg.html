Japanese superhero Chikara Kaminari has accepted her destiny: to save mankind from powerful political extremists. Joined by her friends, the empathic Renka and the shadow-controlling Gen, Chikara journeys to America to rescue her friend Michiko from the Ayn Rand-obsessed billionaire, Chillingworth. As they search for their adversaries, Chikara and her friends encounter a murderous cell of homegrown terrorists called RAMPAGE. This militia of neo-nazis, white supremacists and anti-government extremists will stop at nothing to bring down the government, and only the three heroes can stop them before their extremism claims innocent lives. The future hangs in the balance as Chillingworth lures the world's most powerful leaders to the United Nations. Using Michiko's mind-controlling voice, he seeks to usher in a new era of selfishness and anarchy. The heroes must put aside their disagreements and work together before America's most extreme ideologues tear the world apart.
