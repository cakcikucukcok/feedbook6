Tujunga is a romantic mystery that explores the magic of love and the hardships required to sustain it against intolerance. Two love stores are pulled together into a shocking ending.

Xochi Arias liked to unravel poetic allegories, an entertaining parallel to uncovering secrets in her viral DNA research. On her accidental death, her grieving fiancé, Robert Corelli, suspect she has been murdered. From the allegories inside a treasured book of poems, he is directed as if by spiritual guidance, in a cross-country journey to discover the truth.

The romantic story of Courtney McKillon, a test pilot assigned to a covert space mission, and his wife Claudia becomes revealed. In the backdrop of the Cold War in space exploration, their passionate silence on a forbidden love became a secret the National Reconnaissance Office mandated to be kept at any cost. Robert must decide between honoring their silence or claiming revenge.
