Eighteen-year-old Iren Cunningham is quickly approaching manhood and on the verge of gaining more freedom than he has ever known. But after he chooses to attend Morehouse College, his mother, Dessie, worries that his handsome looks and headstrong attitude would cause him not to take school too seriously. Iren and his best friend, Rob, head to Atlanta, Georgia, with hopes of making their dreams come true. Rob is a basketball player who wants to be eventually scouted for a spot in the NBA. Irens goal is less future-orientedhe wants to chill with as many women as he can. But when Iren meets the beautiful and mysterious Christina, he soon realizes that the things he once cared about now hold little meaning. When a medical crisis in Christinas family threatens to sever their budding romance, Iren is prepared to fight to keep this new relationship from fading. In this tale of unbridled lust and passion, all hell breaks loose as Iren, his family, and friends are about to learn that some secrets should have never been kept.
