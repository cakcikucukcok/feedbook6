WHAT THE WERE-BEAR WANTS

Jared Ursa has been hunted all his life, simply for being born a shifter, and like every male in his family, he can transform into a giant black bear at will.

Hiding out in the woods, he crosses paths with Diana, a huntress dedicated to bringing him in. Sexy, sultry, and definitely dangerous, Diana makes a formidable foe.

But their face-to-face encounter changes the whole perspective of their relationship: from hunter and hunted to sexy, steamy lovers.

She’s entranced by his passionate, predatory nature tempered by a natural gentility; he can’t stop thinking about how she’s the first woman not to be afraid of him...and his size...

THE BEAR STOLE MY HEART

Won’t you come protect me?

All her life, Jenny’s been an accidental troublemaker: a good girl playing the role of a brat. What she needs, her family decides, is to figure things out on her own. And so Jenny is forced to swap her high-flying city life for a more quiet living out in the woods.

Enter Conrad, the dark and daring lumberjack living next door, whose gentle sensibilities leaves him surprised by her spoiled upper-class attitude.

As they come to terms with each other, their attraction is very real — but little does Jenny know, Conrad’s protectiveness and chivalrous nature comes from his grizzly-sized secret: the well-endowed lumberjack is also a primal bear shifter.

Will Conrad’s desire to make Jenny his mate be too much, too soon for the clueless city girl? Or will Jenny acknowledge her need for an alpha male who’ll help her get back on her feet?
