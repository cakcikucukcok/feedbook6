Winter and Landon were destined for each other. When Winter first laid eyes on Landon, stars had aligned. They were both raised for royalty, but a war was brewing overhead. When Winter's parents disappeared, she was forced to take charge. Not long after gaining the crown, Landon was forced to fight in war and she was left to defend herself; that is until she started receiving strange letters. That's when she meets Vladimir, the young leader of the Black Deaths who had been hidden from view for nearly twenty years. To Winter, he was venom in a golden chalice but she wanted to drink from him as if he were the elixir of life. One day Winter disappeared from Vladimir's life. Centuries passed. Vladimir never gave up looking for Winter, but what he found was something unsuspected. This enchanting tale tells the story of two passionate rulers.When the battle arrives on their homefront, they are forced to make a decision. Will they sacrifice everything for one another, or will it be the end of this forbidden love?
