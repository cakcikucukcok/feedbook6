An Atlantean civilization engulfed by cataclysm falls through time and space, scattering hundreds of thousands of souls throughout the dimensional fabric of Free Will Universe One. The survivors, trapped on Earth for eons with no way out, attempt to rebuild a Civilization of Light on a dense and hostile planet. Failed retrieval missions leave Earth poised for auction to the highest bidder. Leaders of the Founder Races, the Elohim, and the Seraphiam discover a single timeline of redemptiona brief window at the end of Earths final Ascension cycle. As the Galactic Federations prepare the planet for a transfer of ownership, they must leverage their human incarnates, tasking them to step into unseen realms and forge a collaborative rescue mission against all odds. Told from the perspective of galactic observers charting the orchestrated evolution of man, this cleverly crafted fictional narrative draws from channeled information and presents compelling evidence revealing the higher truth of humanitys origins and Earths destiny.
