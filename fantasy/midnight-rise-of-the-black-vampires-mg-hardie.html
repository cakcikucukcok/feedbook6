Midnight: Rise of the Black Vampires is a modern, multicultural twist on vampire stories of old 

 

Ambrosia Wharton is a little faster and a little stronger than her classmates. She has all the problems of the average teenager, except for her daily need to consume blood.

Ambrosia Wharton, Amber, is a vampire.

Vampires have remained hidden from humans for good reason, and it's not easy to keep a growing race of blood drinking beings secret in the middle of a metropolis.

Amber loves her father, but his odd habits have always been a mystery. But that all changes after her nightmares start. Nightmares brought on by puberty force her to live with her mother in Angel Beach, California...Amber's dilemma is that she hasn't received her powers yet. 

The nightmares give Amber a glimpse of a dark and twisted side of being a vampire, a side that no one ever mentioned. Amber has a new school, new friends and has a host of new emotions but there is something about Adrian that draws her to him.

Midnight: Rise of the Black Vampires is part Graphic Novel, part Novel and all Vampire. The 2nd Edition has specifically colored text, artwork and a section for coloring.
