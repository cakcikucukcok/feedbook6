By definition, a virtual team is a group of individuals who work across time, space and organisational boundaries with links strengthened by webs of communication technology. Business should be running smooth once you’ve formed your own team. The reality often falls short. Poor communication and incompetence can turn your dream team into a nightmare. Now imagine having a workforce where everyone steps up and is constantly making things happen.
