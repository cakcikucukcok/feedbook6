Maybe you've been there. You have certainly seen it: online reviews and posts designed by the authors to destroy a business. If you haven't been the victim of such posts, you almost certainly will be. Your online reputation is critical to your practice success, and you need to take active steps to promote and manage an excellent online reputation. Word of Mouse Marketing for Dentists will show you how to establish an ongoing reputation program to protect your practice from online trolls and those patients who are impossible to please.
