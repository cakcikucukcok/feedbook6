He knew he had seen something, but never the likes of this. What could it be? And then he heard a noise “Wooooshshshsaaaa.” …The bright yellow school bus rumbled up Highway 6 being filled with trouble makers, 20 pre-adolescent kids who are away from their parents, many for the first time, 2 camp counsellors and 1 sympathetic bus driver... So far, the kid’s lives had been difficult. All 20 of them, like others before every summer, were allowed to go to the free camp, if they qualified as living with a long term sickness … The kids had not yet won the lottery of an easy life, but they had the lottery of being selected to go to camp this summer, which was a small victory and any victory provided hope. …The bus driver, Clayton, smiled. “One day they will surprise you, maybe save the world, or even you.” …The captain (of the ferry) made a rare public address announcement. “Your attention please.
