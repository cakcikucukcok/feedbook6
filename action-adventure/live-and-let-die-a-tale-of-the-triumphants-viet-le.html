Targeted for the young adult (YA) audience. Live and Let Die: A Tale of the Triumphants is a fiction Novel by Viet Le who is a first time inspiring author with a powerful message in this imaginative fictional story filled with emotions and surprises. This epic story will bring you back to the feel good era where adventure and innocence were once found in the retrospective golden stages before adulthood. This book is targeted for young adults and the sci-fi audience where fantasy embarks a new generation of lost readers, hoping to draw in a new generation of inspired readers. Also by purchasing this book, you will be contributing by helping in donating to the Aflac Cancer & Blood Disorders Center Children Healthcare of Atlanta. The reason for this project is to raise awareness and help the children who suffer through cancer every day. Viet Le has lost his father through cancer and thought he would dedicate his life in helping those who needs it most. Proceeds from this project will benefit Children Healthcare of Atlanta. We can make a difference.
