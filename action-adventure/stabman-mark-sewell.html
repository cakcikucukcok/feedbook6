The city reels under a wave of violent crimewhat would you do? If youre like Stabman, you would stand up and oppose it. And if youre really like Stabman, you would probably go about it all wrong, especially at first.

Armed with nothing more than mental imbalance and an unquenchable thirst for Justice, Stabman is a one-man wrecking ball aimed at the dark heart of criminal scum across the land. Understanding and rehabilitation are not words in his vocabulary, but ultra-violence most certainly is.

Dark, violent, and surprisingly funny in places, this is a journey into the mind of someone psychotic enough to take the fight to scum on their own terms, only at a level they cannot comprehend. Join Stabmanif you dareas he bungles and murders his way to full hero status. This isnt your typical superhero storynot recommended for timid readers.
