Combine Your Favorite Pre-Cut Fabrics to Make 14 Gorgeous Quilts!

Discover a world of exciting design possibilities by blending different types and sizes of pre-cut fabrics to create stunning quilts in less time. With instructions for 14 quilts and 5 variations, you'll be inspired to start sewing with your favorite fabric lines and pre-cut stash. As a bonus, each pattern is also fat quarter friendly, featuring instructions on how to use fat quarters instead of pre-cut fabrics to make each project.

Inside, you'll find:

  • 14 quilts and 5 variations featuring standard piecing as well as applique and paper piecing
  • Optional fat quarter cutting instructions for each quilt
  • Clear diagrams and detailed instructions that make sewing easy
  • An inspiration gallery to spark your own creative designs
With the lush selection of pre-cut fabrics available, you'll love combining your favorite colors and cuts to make Pre-Cut Combo Quilts!
