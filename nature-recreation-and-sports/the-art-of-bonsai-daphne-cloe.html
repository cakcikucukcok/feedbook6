Bonsai is the art of creating miniature trees, cultivating them for years in a small vase.

With this particular technique directs the plant during the growth process, to assume the shapes and dimensions required, while respecting balance completely vegetative and functional.

There is talk of bonsai art, as do bonsai is an art that involves various knowledge, both in the general field of botany, and in the more particular techniques bonsai.

All these skills are applied to grow a plant that meets certain aesthetic.

Another interesting aspect is that it is a work of art never finished: the plant continues to grow and change, so you must always look after her.
