A sailing adventure! Capt. Boyd Chailland goes undercover to sail, fly, fight and solve a mystery that mixes jihad, the diamond business, crooked bankers and new discoveries in vaccine technology. Someone has spent millions to make a vaccine for Ebola, the world's deadliest virus; and has kept it a secret. Why? Strategic Command's Counter Proliferations Task Force assumes the worst and re-calls Chailland, their "go to" guy for "black ops off the books" to find out. Leading a team of quirky misfits, Boyd struggles to get ahead of the trail of deceit and death that leads to Chardonnay, a century old sailing yacht owned by notorious European merchant banker Michelle Meilland, and a trans-Atlantic crossing in hurricane season. The kick-ass climax in the Azores will leave the reader breathless, but it's not over. Villains and heroes abound in this story; all are well intentioned, all are flawed. But there is only one Devil on Chardonnay.
