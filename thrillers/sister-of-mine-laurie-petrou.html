  • SAVVY SELF-PROMOTER AND TRAINED PUBLIC SPEAKER: Laurie received her PhD in Communication and Culture from Ryerson and York Universities, and teaches topics surrounding digital media, gender, and representation. She is a TEDx speaker, and the creator and co-creator of a number of multimedia works including audio, interactive and video pieces.

  • SMALL TOWN SUSPENSE: Readers are asking for more domestic suspense featuring twisted families and Laurie delivers with Sister of Mine, which follows two secret-keeping sisters living on the outskirts of a small, gossip-driven town and driven to terrible acts.
