Thirteen unpredictable short stories of intrigue, murder, mystery and revenge. Where secrets are not always safe. Things are not as they seem. And revenge can be sweet. These stories prove that emotions can drive people to extremes. The mind can play tricks on you. And reality is often in the eye of the beholder.

Meet characters that appear to be normal, in situations that are anything but usual. How far will they go for the life they desire? Why did Lana wait three days to tell police her newlywed husband was missing? How far will Connor go to win his Uncle’s inheritance? Where’s a good place to hide an unexpectedly dead body? Is 20 years too long to wait for revenge? Can a dead husband still wash the dishes? 

As secrets force their way to the surface, these tales will keep you guessing to the end.
