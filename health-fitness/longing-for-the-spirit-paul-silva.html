How do you discover your true self?

 

    Is the prison we condemned ourselves to destined to determine our fate? Do our childhood fears and self-doubt manage to worm into us so deeply that we can never extricate ourselves from them, standing forever under the stern disapproval of our past?

 

This book builds upon the foundation of these questions to construct a story-mosaic of one life - Paul’s life - but it doesn't end there. Wishful thinking and insecurity never deliver the dreams we so badly teared for as children. Only honestly, persistence, endurance, acceptance, hope and love can provide the spirits we thirst after. Paul write with clever precision to uncover just how he found himself again. In reading this book, you too will readily see the ways you can realize your dreams.
