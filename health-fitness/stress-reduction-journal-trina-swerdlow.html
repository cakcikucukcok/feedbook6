Are You Ready to Meditate and Journal Your Way to Better Health? This Stress Reduction Journal will assist you in managing stress from the inside out. Through meditating and journaling, you'll acquire skills for calming the tensions of both your mind and body. Numerous scientific studies provide concrete evidence of the physical and psychological benefits that regular meditation offers. These studies are based on the same meditation method that is taught in this book. The health benefits of meditation include: significant lowering of blood pressure; reducing stress and stress-related diseases; as well as, decreasing anxiety and depression. Journaling is another effective method for lowering stress and improving your health.
