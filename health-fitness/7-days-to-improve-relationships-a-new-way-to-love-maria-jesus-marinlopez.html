Every one of us wants to have a happy, harmonious, long lasting relationship. What prevents this are our unconscious patterns. We act these out in our romantic relationships more so than in any other form. From the beginning of our life the way we interact creates a set of limiting unconscious patterns and beliefs. We can be trapped in unconscious patterns, but what gives us our power is, that when we come to full awareness, then we don't need to react, but can act from an empowered place. We have the ability to choose and as a result of making these choices, we create the life that we were born to live. Enjoy this 7 day challenge to improve your relationships
