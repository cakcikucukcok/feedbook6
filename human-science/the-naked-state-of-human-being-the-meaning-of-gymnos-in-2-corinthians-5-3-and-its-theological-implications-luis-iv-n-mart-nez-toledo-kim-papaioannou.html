This book explains the image of nakedness (gymnos) taken by Paul in the Corinthian correspondence to refer to the state of human being during death. Through an academic approach, but simple language, the author explains the biblical monist understanding of human being. This book uncovers the ancient problem of the continuation of personal identity from life prior to death, on to resurrection.
