Jimmy McGee has fallen into a state of despair, still confused and traumatized by his unexpected encounter with the opposite sex. Frustrated with Billys wild behavior, Jimmy creates a distance between them that ultimately leads to conflict. Jimmys straight-laced behavior only reinforces Billys belief that Jimmy is and always has been the favored son.
