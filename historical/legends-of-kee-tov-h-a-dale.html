Legends of Kee Tov has risen out of a place in the northern Minnesota wilderness. On this spot along a river flowing into a large lake, my family and I built our cabins. The burial grounds of the ancient Laurel culture are very near. When I sense a soft wind blowing, I ask questions, and I am whispered into the ages, where I come to know and love the beautiful people who first made their dwellings here. From them I receive answers to my questions.

The lives of the young and old from various clans collide and mesh into this collection of stories that is sure to captivate and connect with readers of all ages.
